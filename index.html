Movies Should Be Fun

Movie Dash is a simple web service that cuts out the hardest part about going to the movies. Don't worry about choosing which theater to go to, or working out when to leave the house. Just enter the time you want to take off, and Movie Dash will show you which movies are starting at the perfect time.

Reliable, Modern Technology

Movie Dash is built on the MEAN stack--MongoDB, Express.js, Angular.js, and Node.js. This flexible Javascript-based technology suite powers Movie Dash and many of the most popular and powerful apps on the web.
